About Us

Who doesn't love the beautiful game

Our website is dedicated to providing comprehensive information about injured players within the Big Six clubs. We understand the importance of staying updated on the fitness status of players, as it impacts their performance and the outcomes of the matches. Whether you are an avid fan, a fantasy football enthusiast, or a sports bettor, our goal is to be your go-to source for all the latest updates on injuries, recovery progress, expected return dates, and more. We strive to deliver accurate and reliable information, keeping you informed so that you can make informed decisions. With our user-friendly interface and regularly updated content, you can trust us to be your ultimate resource for tracking the fitness of players in the Big Six clubs and ensuring you never miss a beat.

What is Big Six?

"Big Six" includes Manchester United, Liverpool, Arsenal, Chelsea, Manchester City and Tottenham.



Why care about injuries?

Injuries are sometimes a turning point for players in their careers, and sometimes it is important for the fans to know about it and understand it. Fans show a lot of support and they deserve to know about the injuries. We as fans have to support them at all times and should let them know our interest in seeing them back on the pitch. This website is made only for the those really care about the players and want to know about their injuries. We provide you with their injury information, type of injury, injury reason, expected recovery date, etc.
